The UF Roblox Lab is a semester-long initiative I lead through GatorAI. The goal is to explore how large language models can assist in teaching game development - specifically on the Roblox platform using Lua/Luau.

## The Idea

Most students in the club have programming experience but haven't touched game dev before. LLM tools like Claude Code, Cursor, and Gemini CLI can dramatically lower the barrier to entry by helping students scaffold projects, debug Luau scripts, and understand Roblox Studio's API.

## How It Works

Students form small teams and build Roblox games over the course of the semester. They're encouraged to use AI coding assistants as part of their workflow, and we track how it affects their learning curve and output quality compared to traditional tutorials.

## My Role

As Applied Lead for GatorAI, I organized the project structure, recruited participants, and provided mentorship on Roblox development patterns - drawing on years of professional experience building on the platform through VuPlay Creative.
