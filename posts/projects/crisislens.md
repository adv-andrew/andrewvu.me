CrisisLens was built at Hacklytics 2026 for the Databricks x UN Geo-Insight Challenge. It's an interactive tool that visualizes where humanitarian funding doesn't match the actual severity of crises around the world.

## The Overlooked Crisis Index

The core of the project is a composite metric we called the "Overlooked Crisis Index." It combines multiple data sources - conflict intensity, displacement numbers, food insecurity, and funding allocations - to surface crises that are severe but underfunded relative to their impact.

## Tech

- Databricks for data processing and analytics
- Interactive mapping and visualization layer
- Composite scoring algorithm for ranking overlooked crises

The project highlights gaps in the global humanitarian response by making the data accessible and visual, rather than buried in spreadsheets and reports.
