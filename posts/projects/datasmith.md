DataSmith was a pitch developed for NexHack 2026. The concept is an AI-powered tool that streamlines common data science workflows - cleaning, transformation, feature engineering, and exploratory analysis - by letting users describe what they want in natural language.

## The Problem

Data scientists spend a huge chunk of their time on repetitive preprocessing tasks. DataSmith aims to automate the boring parts so you can focus on the interesting parts: modeling, analysis, and insight.

## The Pitch

We presented DataSmith as a tool that sits between raw data and your notebook. You describe the transformation you want, and it generates and executes the pandas/SQL code for you, with full transparency into what it's doing.

The project was a great exercise in product thinking and technical communication under hackathon time pressure.
