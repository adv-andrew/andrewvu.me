Canvas Pet is a Chrome extension built as a group project for CEN3031 (Software Engineering) at UF. The idea is simple - complete your Canvas assignments on time and your virtual pet thrives. Fall behind, and it suffers.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React with Next.js
- **Backend:** Supabase for auth and database
- **Extension:** Chrome Manifest V3
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS

## How It Works

The extension connects to your Canvas LMS account and tracks assignment due dates and submission status. Your pet's health, happiness, and evolution are tied directly to your academic performance.
