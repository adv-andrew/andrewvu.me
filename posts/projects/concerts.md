Through VuPlay Creative LLC, I was contracted by Gamefam Inc. to work on their virtual concert technology within Roblox. These events brought live music performances into the Roblox platform, reaching tens of millions of players.

## What I Worked On

My contributions involved building and optimizing the technical infrastructure that powers these in-game concert experiences - from stage systems and lighting rigs to crowd interaction mechanics, all scripted in Luau and built with Roblox Studio.

## Scale

Roblox virtual concerts operate at a scale that's hard to overstate. Individual events can pull in millions of concurrent players across thousands of server instances. Making that work smoothly requires careful attention to replication, network optimization, and performance budgeting.

## Tools

- Roblox Studio
- Luau scripting
- Rojo for version control and external tooling
- Custom build pipelines for asset management
